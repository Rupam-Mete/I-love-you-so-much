💛 I Miss My Ori 💛

I broke down... I didn’t mean to raise my voice... I just missed you so much... Tomar obhiman, tomar nibraash... amar jibon e oi sob cheye beshi guruttopurno... I want you to know this — crystal clear: I don’t miss any girl... I miss MY Ori 💫 Not Ori... not a name... Amar Ori... 💭❤ Please forgive me... for missing the signs, for not catching your gentle hints,,, But next time,,, I swear I’ll look deeper, listen better, love louder... Tomake emon bhabe bhalobashi je... I don't have that much word to speak that I can explan...
